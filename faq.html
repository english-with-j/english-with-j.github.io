Why would I use your service instead of that of Chat GPT to write my CV?

One still needs to be well-versed in LaTeX or HTML in order to tailor to individual needs the content that Chat GPT generates. Moreover, Chat GPT generates only very generic writing that in most cases is not relevant enough to your career.

Why would I use your service instead of that of a CV-generating website?

CV-generating websites provide you with just basic formatting. Such websites do not write your CV for you - you are expected to have your professional summary and other paragraphs of your CV already written in order to generate one for you. Moreover, such websites do not offer much customization. You might be interested in squeezing everything into a one-pager, getting rid of the picture from your CV, etc.
